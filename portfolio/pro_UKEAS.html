UKEAS Boarding School's Website

UKEAS Boarding School's Design Concept

·   ·   ·

Background: UKEAS thought that their website is not elegant and exquisite enough, so they asked me to redesign this website.

Role: Web Design, Front-end Development

Time: 2018/9

Github: UKEAS_project

·   ·   ·

OVERVIEW

This Boarding School's target customers are affluent parents. Therefore, it needs to be elegant, sincere, welcoming, exquisite and professional. There is a psychological research which indicates people are easily attracted by the picture, but what makes them buy or use the service is influenced by narratives. Therefore, in my opinion, narratives are more important than pictures. Moreover, when people see the pictures, we need to make them feel like they are studying abroad. Let them get involved is one way of marketing.
