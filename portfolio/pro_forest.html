Navbar Redesign II

When I was coding and testing the website, I found out that the two navbars are easily to let me confused. The main navbar always exists in the page, but the sub navbar will changed as users clicked on the main navbar. That is to say, main navbar is fixed, and sub navbar is unfixed. Therefore, I draw a line between them and give them different style to seperate them.
