Shake Event

It is a persuasive design. When people don’t need to buy our drinks, they will lack motivation to use our app. Therefore, we design this interactive design as a trigger to our customers. During the event period, they can open our app and then shake their phones, we will give them a coupon of 60%, 50%, 30%, or 20% off according to a certain probability.

It is a cute marketing design which is wrapped in Chinese culture. This event is only in our iOS and Android app, but it is actually a web view. So, it’s designed and hand-coded by me.
