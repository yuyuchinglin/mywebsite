Business Goal - Challenge & Solution

Challenge
According to our POS system, we have found out that customers who come to our tea shop 80% are new guests and 20% are old guests. This app’s business goal is to make new guests come more often. Our challenges is that
1. Appeal to new users
2. Make users come more often

Solution
To appeal to new users, we use coupons, so that they can try our drinks at a lower price. What's more, to encourage long-term engagement, we use some long-term games such as a lottery game and location check-in, which needs a long time to achieve.

Competitor Analysis - DaYung's Tea Shop's App

Advangtages
Since the tea shop is very popular and has many branches in Taiwan, they have many loyal customers. The main idea to create this app is a platform for their customers to collect points or see the information about them. To conclude, there are two main advantages:
1. They have many loyal customers.
2. The information is very clear.

Disadvangtages
I think this app is only for long-term or loyalty users. However, since they have a real physical card, it’s not necessary for customers to collect points on the app. Moreover, they do not have any special marketing event to appeal to new or potential customers. To sum up, there are two disadvantages:
1. It’s not very necessary for long-term or loyalty users.
2. There are no marketing events to appeal to new or potential customers.

User Profile

Different users would use the app differently. Therefore, I categorized them into two user profiles based on their visit frequency.

1. Old guests - Visit our stores more than three times
Features: often visit China / love the flavor of WLJ / Chinese people
Performed tasks:
- Collect points to get some bonus
- Use the delivery service
- know the latest news or marketing events
- Attend the games to win a prize

2. New guests - Visit our stores less than three times
Features: pass by / appealed by marketing events / like to try new favor
Performed tasks:
- Get coupons to make the drink cheaper

The Interface of Lottery Game

When customers buy our drinks or visit our app, they can receive one point to use in the lottery game. We have plenty of prizes. People who use more points in one prize will get better chances to get the prize. For example, an iPhone needs 30,000 points to draw. If a customer gives one point in this prize, then he will get 1/30000 to get this prize. Because we have many great prizes so it actually scatters the points and takes a very long time to draw.

I have come up with an idea that we need to put a ranking on it. For example, when we play basketball shooting machine, there is a ranking record which we always want to challenge. Likely, we have the same mechanism. We have a top ten ranking of how many points users used on every prize in which makes users compare each other and encourage them to use more points.

Irregular Marketing Event - Shake Your Phones

Our users lacked the motivation to open it except when they wanted to buy drinks. To increase engagement, we created an event period in which users could open our app and win a discount coupon once per day by shaking the phone. During the event period, they can open our app and then shake their phones, we will give them a coupon of 60%, 50%, 30%, or 20% off according to a certain probability. It is a persuasive strategy in interaction design that requires a target group's long-term engagement to encourage customer loyalty.

It is a cute marketing design that is wrapped by traditional Chinese culture. When users shake their phones, an Imperial decree which contains a coupon will expand. This event is actually a web view on our iOS and Android app. So, it’s designed and hand-coded by me.

User Experience in Tea Shop Store

Problem
When there are some marketing events in our app, I often see many people in our store are busy with downloading and using the app. Some people may feel it is insecure or trouble to download an app on their phone. However, we want to make customers feel comfortable and pleasant instead of being pressured to downloading or using our app.

Many people are busy downloading our app to get the coupons during lining up.

Solution
We should not force every new customer to download our app when they come into the store. Instead, if we want to give coupons or discounts to customers, we should give all the customers equally or having some games like Sweepstakes directly in our store.

To cultivate customers to be a user of our app, we can put a sticker on their cups. One cup of drink has only one sticker, which contains a serial number or QR code. Customers can collect points or join marketing events on our app through the sticker.

Put a sticker which contains serial number to register our app to solve this problem.
