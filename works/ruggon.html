IoT Cloud Service

Users can easily organize, update, and monitor their devices remotely and wirelessly.

·   ·   ·

Overview

RuggON Inc. and Ubiqconn Inc. all belong to a Taiwanese established company, First International Computer Company (FIC Inc.). RuggON Inc. sells tablet computers for military and industrial uses, and Ubiqconn Inc. offers software solutions for RuggON’s tablets. For example, Ubiqconn Inc. can build an IoT cloud service for RuggON’s tablets so that people can organize their devices, update software or firmware, and remotely monitor their devices on it.

I work as a UI/UX, front-end designer in Ubiqconn Inc.'s Digital Connectivity Service Division. I am the only designer in the division, so I need to learn how to efficiently express my ideas in UX/UI design with other professions. I have been cooperating with our manager to plan and design an IoT device management cloud service and an Android app on our RuggON tablets.

Company: Ubiqconn + RuggON

Role: UX/UI, Front-end Designer

Time: 2019/3 - NOW (full-time)

Interaction Design (for Internal User)

1. Organize devices, users, and companies
RuggON Inc. sells many tablets to different companies, so managers need a way to organize these devices, users, and companies through this cloud service.

Managers must organize every companies' users and devices.
·   ·   ·

2. Update devices' firmware and software wirelessly
In the past, if managers wanted to update tablets’ firmware, they had to let each tablet connect to the computer one by one. However, this IoT cloud service aims to solve this problem. That is, through our cloud service, managers can upload and update the firmware and software over a network without human-to-human or human-to-computer interaction.

SOTA & FOTA update workflow
·   ·   ·

Interaction Design (for Customers)

This cloud service not only benefits our company’s internal users but also provides a value-added service to our customers who buy our tablets. I categorize these into two main functions and translate them into an introduction and pictures for our customers.

1. Managing devices remotely
It is more convenient for customers to manage your devices remotely. Through our cloud service, they can remotely check their devices’ information or install or remove apps from their devices. Moreover, if they have a fleet of devices, they can easily group them to sync apps.

Managing your devices remotely.
·   ·   ·

2. Having their own app marketplace
Apart from offering our RuggON official apps, customers can now upload their apps and publish them to their devices immediately or at a scheduled time! Furthermore, they do not need to wait for Google’s review every time when updating their apps.

Having your own app marketplace.
·   ·   ·

3. Monitor devices remotely
Our final goal is to become involved in the Internet of Vehicles (IoV), which refers to the network of different entities, such as vehicles, pedestrians, roads, parking lots, and city infrastructure and provides real-time communication between them. Specifically, customers can use our cloud service to monitor their vehicles' information such as oil, electricity, time, position, tires’ status, etc.

Users can monitor their tires’ status on our cloud service.

Project Learnings

Design and development is iterative.
Although I did a lot of preparation for the design and proposed the best possible version, many problems still occurred during and/or after developing the whole website. I changed my prototype many times, and I believe that it is best to think about the big picture while redesigning it. Therefore, I learned that only iterative design can come close to being perfect.

Having ideas or opinions before meetings
Our company holds many meetings. I discovered that if people did not have ideas or opinions beforehand, they will be easily influenced by others’ opinions. This makes it difficult to reach a good conclusion during our discussions, since we do not have enough time or clear thoughts to draw such a conclusion. Therefore, I believe that having ideas or opinions before group discussions is best in order to promote deep and high-quality discussions and conclusions.
