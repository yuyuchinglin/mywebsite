Overview

RuggON Inc. sells tablet computers for military and industrial. Ubiqconn Inc. offers software solutions for RuggON tablets. For example, we build an IoT cloud service for RuggON tablets so that people can organize their devices, update software or firmware and remote monitor their devices on it.

I am UI/UX, front-end designer in the Ubiqconn's Digital Connectivity Service Division. I corporate with our manager to plan and design an IoT device management cloud service and an Android app on the RuggON tablet.

Company: Ubiqconn (software) + RuggON (tablet)

Role: UX/UI, Front-end Designer

Time: 2019/3 - NOW (full-time)

·   ·   ·

Interaction Design

1. Organize devices, users, and companies: RuggON Inc. sells lots of tablets to different companies, so their PM needs to organize and distribute those devices, users, and companies through this cloud service.

PM needs to organize and distribute every companies' users and devices.
·   ·   ·

2. Update devices' firmware and software over-the-air: RuggON's Inc. need to update their devices' firmware and software remotely. That is, through this cloud service, PM/customer can upload and update their devices' firmware and software without connecting them to computers.

SOTA & FOTA update workflow
·   ·   ·

3. Monitor devices remotely: Users can monitor devices' information such as oil, electricity, time, position, and tiring condition, etc. through the cloud service. We will add more features in the future.

Users can monitor the tiring conditions on our cloud service.
