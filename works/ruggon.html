IoT Cloud Service

Users can easily organize, update and monitor their devices.

Overview

RuggON Inc. and Ubiqconn Inc. all belong to a Taiwanese established company, First International Computer Company (FIC Inc.). RuggON Inc. sells tablet computers for military and industrial. Ubiqconn Inc. Ubiqconn Inc. offers software solutions to RuggON tablets. For example, we build an IoT cloud service for RuggON tablets so that people can organize their devices, update software or firmware and remote monitor their devices on it.

I work as UI/UX, front-end designer in the Ubiqconn's Digital Connectivity Service Division. I am the only designer on the division, so I need to learn how to express my ideas in UX/UI design with other professions efficiently. I corporate with our manager to plan and design an IoT device management cloud service and an Android app on our RuggON tablets.

Company: Ubiqconn + RuggON

Role: UX/UI, Front-end Designer

Time: 2019/3 - NOW (full-time)

Interaction Design

1. Organize devices, users, and companies
Since RuggON Inc. sells lots of tablets to different companies, managers or customers need a way to organize these devices, users, and companies through this cloud service.

Managers need to organize every companies' users and devices.
·   ·   ·

2. Update devices' firmware and software over-the-air
In the past, if managers/customers want to update tablets’ firmware, they need to let the tablets connect to the computer one by one. This IoT cloud service aims to solve this problem. That is, through our cloud service, users can upload and update the firmware and software over a network without requiring human-to-human or human-to-computer interaction.

SOTA & FOTA update workflow
·   ·   ·

3. Monitor devices remotely
Our final goal is the Internet of Vehicle(IoV), which refers to the network of different entities, such as vehicles, pedestrians, roads, parking lots and city infrastructure and provides real-time communication among them. To be more specific, users can monitor devices' information such as oil, electricity, time, position, tiring condition, etc. in our cloud service.

Users can monitor the tiring conditions on our cloud service.

Project Learnings

Design is iterative
Although I did a lot of preparation for the design and proposed the best possible version, many problems still occurred during and/or after developing the whole website. I changed my prototype many times, and I believe that it is best to think about the big picture while redesigning it. Therefore, I learned that only iterative design can come close to being perfect.

Having ideas or opinions before meetings
Our company holds many meetings. I discovered that if people did not have ideas or opinions beforehand, they will be easily influenced by others’ opinions. This makes it difficult to reach a good conclusion during our discussions, since we do not have enough time or clear thoughts to draw such a conclusion. Therefore, I believe that having ideas or opinions before group discussions is best in order to promote deep and high-quality discussions and conclusions.
