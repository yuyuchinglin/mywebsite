IoT Device Management

Users can remotely organize, update, and monitor their devices at scale.

·   ·   ·

Overview

RuggON Inc. and Ubiqconn Inc. are owned by First International Computer Company (FIC Inc.), a Taiwanese founded company. RuggON Inc. sells tablet computers for military and industrial purposes, and Ubiqconn Inc. offers software solutions for RuggON tablets. For example, we built an IoT device management cloud service for RuggON tablets so that people can organize their devices, update software or firmware, and remotely monitor their devices.

I work as a UI/UX, front-end designer in Ubiqconn's Digital Connectivity Service Division. I am the only designer in the division, so it’s important that I can express my UX/UI designs with my colleagues in layman’s terms that they can understand. I worked with our manager to plan and design an IoT device management cloud service and an Android application for our RuggON tablets.

Company: Ubiqconn + RuggON

Role: UX/UI, Front-end Designer

Time: 2019/3 - Recent (full-time)

Interaction Design (for Internal User)

1. Organize devices, users, and companies
Since RuggON Inc. sells many tablets to different companies, managers need a way to organize devices, users, and companies through the cloud service.

Managers need to organize every company's users and devices.
·   ·   ·

2. Update devices' firmware and software remotely
In the past, if managers wanted to update the tablets’ firmware, they needed to connect the tablets to the computer one at a time. This IoT cloud service was created to solve this problem by allowing managers to upload and update the firmware (Firmware over-the-air, FOTA) and software (Software over-the-air, SOTA) over a network without requiring a physical connection.

SOTA & FOTA update workflow
·   ·   ·

Interaction Design (for Customers)

This cloud service is not only for our company’s internal users but also for the consumers who buy our tablets. This service has three main functions for consumers.

1. Manage devices remotely
Customers can conveniently manage their devices remotely. Through our cloud service, customers can remotely check their devices’ information as well as install or remove applications on their devices. Additionally, they can sync applications over multiple devices.

Manage devices remotely
·   ·   ·

2. Having their own app marketplace
In addition to our RuggON official applications, customers can now upload applications to their devices and publish them immediately or at a scheduled time! Furthermore, they don’t need to submit your applications to be reviewed by Google every time they update them.

Having their own app marketplace
·   ·   ·

3. Monitor devices remotely
Our final goal is the Internet of Vehicle (IoV), which enables real-time communication between different entities such as vehicles, pedestrians, roads, parking lots, and city infrastructure. More specifically, users can monitor their device’s information such as oil, electricity, time, position, tire-pressure, etc. in our device management cloud service.

Tire-pressure monitoring system

Project Learnings

Design and development is iterative.
Although I did a lot of preparation for the design and proposed the best possible version, many problems still occurred during and/or after developing the whole website. I changed my prototype many times, and I believe that it is best to think about the big picture while redesigning it. Therefore, I learned that only iterative design can come close to being perfect.

Having ideas or opinions before meetings
Our company holds many meetings. I discovered that if people did not have ideas or opinions beforehand, they will be easily influenced by others’ opinions. This makes it difficult to reach a good conclusion during our discussions, since we do not have enough time or clear thoughts to draw such a conclusion. Therefore, I believe that having ideas or opinions before group discussions is best in order to promote deep and high-quality discussions and conclusions.
